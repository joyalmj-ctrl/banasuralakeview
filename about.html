Our Story

Born from a love for the Western Ghats and a belief in slow, mindful living.

At Banasura Lake View, we set out to build a place where the forest leads. Our cottages use locally sourced materials and natural ventilation to reduce energy use. Water is harvested and reused for the gardens. Meals are prepared with seasonal ingredients, many from nearby farms.

We keep things simple, quiet, and human. Wake to birdsong, walk through spice plantations, and end your day under a sky of stars. We welcome families, solo travelers, and seekers of stillness.
